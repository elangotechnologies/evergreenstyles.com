about us

The EverGreen Creations is an upcoming garment industry based in Tiruppur, Tamilnadu, India. Since 2015, we’ve provided customers with the latest fashion collections, as well as pieces from past collections at discounted prices. Our business lies in the intersection of service and luxury, and has consistently kept a high profile among suppliers and buyers alike. We act as small scale industry to provide service to large scale industries in terms of bulk dress making from their un-stitched dress materials. We are also open to get the orders to make custom dresses from any customers.
