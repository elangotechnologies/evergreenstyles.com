about us

The EverGreen Creations garment unit based in Tiruppur, Tamilnadu, India Founded by Azhagesh Kaliyappan . since 2015, we have provided customers with minimum quantity of 500 pieces to 1000 pieces of dress materials. The latest fashion collections are knitted T-Shirt, Polo Shirts, Crew Neck, V-Neck, Pyjamas, Leggings, Tanktop, Sleepwear and Sportswear. We use a range of fabric structures such as Single jersey, Interlock, Rib, fleece, PolyCotton and Cotton Viscose. we are also open to get the orders to make knitted cotton custom dress from any customers.
